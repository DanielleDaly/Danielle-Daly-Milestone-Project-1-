Fingal Farm

Farm to fork, local, fresh and chemical free produce!

Welome to Fingal Farm!

Sustainability and regenerative agriculture is at the heart of what we do. We work in harmony with nature and to provide food as it should be, healthy, fresh and in season.

Our Ethos

We are your local farmer and believe eyeryone has the right to know where their food comes from and how it is produced. Located in north Dublin our objective is to provide organic, chemical free and ethically produced food that values plants animals and nature.

Our Products

We supply a large range of organic fruit and vegetables that changes with the seasons. Our animals are ethically raised, slow grown, fed on a natual diet and free from antibiotics. All our eggs are free-range, pasture raised and chemical free.

Where to buy

We are available at a number of locations throughout Dublin for collection. Pre-order online or by phone for collection at a location and time that suits you. We also attend a number of farmers markets throughout the week.

Sustainability

Working in tune with nature is at the heart of what we do. All our produce is 100% organic. We are a chemical free farm so your food will not contain any nasty stuff. We have a mixed farm system that focuses on regenerative agriculure and protects the soil for generations to come. Our animals are ethically raised and free from antibiotics. Buying from us means food with no air miles, zero plastic and as it's guaranteed Irish you are supporting your local community as well.
