Our Products

Here at Fingal Farm we supply a range of 100% Organic Vegetables and Pasture Raised Eggs. All our produce is chemical, antibiotic and plastic free.

For Veg-boxes pre-order on our website or by phone and collect at one of our 4 locations in North Dublin. Individual items are also available to purchase on the day - no need to pre-order. Eggs can also be pre-ordered but we will have extra available at the collection points as well.

Collection point locations: Malahide, Donabate, Skerries & Raheny.

Vegetables

Fingal's Finest Pasture Raised Eggs

Our hens are raised naturally, on 100% chemical free pastures and are free to roam and forage. Their diet is supplemented with 100% organic GMO free grains and they are not routinely treated with antibiotics. This is an ethical egg enterprise where the hens welfare is at the forefront of what we do.

We supply eggs in a variety of sizes: cartons of 6, 12 and 24 or trays of 30 eggs.
