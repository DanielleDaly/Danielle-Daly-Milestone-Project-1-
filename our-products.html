Fingal's Finest Pasture Raised Eggs

Our hens are raised naturally, on 100% chemical free pastures and are free to roam about as they choose. They are happy and spend their days hunting for bugs and pecking at grasses. Their diet is supplemented with 100% organic GMO free grains. This is an ethical egg enterprise where the hens welfare is at the forefront of what we do.

We supply eggs in a variety of sizes: cartons of 6, 12 and 24 or a tray of 30 eggs.
